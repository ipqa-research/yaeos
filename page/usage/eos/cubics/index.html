PSRK

PSRK

The PSRK EoS [3,4] (Predictive Soave-Redlich-Kwong) is a Soave-Redlich-Kwong cubic equation of state that uses the MHV mixrule and the AlphaMathiasCopeman function. With the MHV mixrule, a Gibbs excess energy model is required, in the case of PSRK, the PSRK-UNIFAC model is used.
