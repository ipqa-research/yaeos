private subroutine pressure(eos, n, V, T, P, dPdV, dPdT, dPdn)

Calculate pressure.

Calculate pressure using residual helmholtz models.

Examples

eos = PengRobinson76(Tc, Pc, w)

n = [1.0_pr, 1.0_pr]
T = 300.0_pr
V = 1.0_pr

call eos%pressure(n, V, T, P, dPdV=dPdV, dPdT=dPdT, dPdn=dPdn)
